### Recommended Sizes
- Dashboard card: 400x300
- Full chart: 800x600
- Inline stat: 200x100
- Social media: 1080x1080

### Format
- SVG preferred (scalable)
- PNG for complex graphics
- WebP for web optimization

### Accessibility
- Sufficient color contrast
- Alternative text ready
- Pattern alternatives to color
- Clear labels always
