# Landing Page Hero Image Prompts

## Template Structure
[SUBJECT] [STYLE] [MOOD] [TECHNICAL SPECS]

---

## General Hero Template
{subject} landing page hero image, modern web design aesthetic, professional, clean composition, high quality, vibrant colors, {mood} atmosphere, suitable for website header, 16:9 aspect ratio, sharp focus, balanced lighting

## Style Modifiers
- Modern: clean lines, minimal clutter, contemporary feel
- Professional: polished, trustworthy, business-appropriate
- Playful: bright colors, dynamic elements, energetic
- Elegant: sophisticated, luxury feel, refined details
- Bold: high contrast, striking visuals, attention-grabbing

## Technical Specifications
- Resolution: 1920x1080 (Full HD) or 2560x1440 (2K)
- Aspect Ratio: 16:9 for standard, 21:9 for ultra-wide
- Format: PNG for transparency, JPG for photos, WebP for web
- Quality: High detail, sharp focus, proper exposure

## Usage Tips
1. Always include "suitable for website" in prompts
2. Specify color schemes that match brand identity
3. Include mood descriptors (professional, friendly, innovative)
4. Mention "clean composition" for better layouts
5. Add "high quality" or "detailed" for better results
